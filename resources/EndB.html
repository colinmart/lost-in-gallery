The stairs never end.

No matter how long you walk, you never seem to make any progress; only an endless diving spiral into an infinite black.

But just as you feel like your legs are about to give out, you realize you can see it, just barely. The bottom of the chasm.
There's nothing there but a doorway. Readying your blade, you take a deep breath and walk around the corner.

It's another gallery.

Its somber blue-lacquered rooms stretch out without revealing any destination, artificial roses sprouting from cracks in the walls.
It's decorated with paintings and sculptures you've never seen before, host to strange back-rooms and twisting corridors
that defy conventional physics.
You can see something moving, deep within.

But without a moment of hesitation, you find yourself crossing the threshold, still grinning gently.
Your hand tightens around your palette knife, red paint steadily dripping from its tip.

If any more monsters come for you, within this fathomless abyss...
